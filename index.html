About ligae hardware
Home of all your building materials.

Formed in 2010 ligae hardware store has been the go to place for genuine hardware tools and all construction related equipment. Located at kigamboni kibada centre area ligae supplies its materials in and around the kigamboni municipal.

Ligae hardware store offers variety of materials electric materials such as: electric cables, sockets, switches, plumbing materials such as: taps, tanks water pipes and all general construction materials such as: wood, cement, gypsum and paints.
